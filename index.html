About Seablast

Hi, I’m Sebastian – aka Seabass! I founded Seablast Swim School in 2023 and am currently the head swim instructor. I’ve been swimming since I was 4 years old, learning through many of the same techniques I now use to teach my students today.

With over six years of experience as a certified Red Cross Water Safety Instructor (WSI) and StarGuard Instructor, and three years as an Ocean Lifeguard, I bring valuable experience in water safety, rescue training, and emergency response to every lesson.

As a bilingual Spanish speaker, I’m proud to offer a welcoming, inclusive, and confidence-building environment for swimmers of all ages and backgrounds.

Swim Lessons

Our swim lessons are designed to promote water safety, build confidence, and make learning fun! We offer private, semi-private, group, and open water lessons for ages 2+. From learning how to float to getting ocean-ready, every lesson is tailored to your comfort levels and personal goals. Using hands-on, encouraging teaching methods, every swimmer will leave each lesson feeling more confident than the last.

If you have access to a pool, we’re happy to bring lessons to you! We will work together to create a flexible schedule that works best.

No pool? No problem! I offer private and semi-private lessons at a pool located in the North of Montana area of Santa Monica. Due to high demand and limited space, it’s important to schedule in advance.

Lessons range from 30 minutes to an hour, based on your goals and preferences. From one-on-one lessons to a small group, we’ll collaborate to build a schedule and lesson plan for you.

Adult Programs

Our Adult Programs are fully customizable for every skill level. From beginners to experienced athletes, we’ve got a lesson for you.

For ocean swimmers and triathletes, we offer specialized open water training focused on wave survival, technique refinement, and confidence-building in unpredictable environments.

For beginners, we focus on learning water fundamentals and building your confidence in the water. We’ll meet you where you’re comfortable and help you grow as a swimmer at your own pace– no pressure, just progress.
