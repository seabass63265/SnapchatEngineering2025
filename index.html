About Seablast

Hi, I’m Sebastian – aka Seabass! I founded Seablast Swim School in 2023 and am currently the head swim instructor. I’ve been swimming since I was 4 years old, learning through many of the same techniques I now use to teach my students today.

With over six years of experience as a certified Red Cross Water Safety Instructor (WSI) and StarGuard Instructor, and three years as an Ocean Lifeguard, I bring valuable experience in water safety, rescue training, and emergency response to every lesson.

As a bilingual Spanish speaker, I’m proud to offer a welcoming, inclusive, and confidence-building environment for swimmers of all ages and backgrounds.

Swim Lessons

At Seablast, our swim lessons are built to encourage safety, confidence, and fun in the water. We teach kids as young as 2 all the way up to adults who are either learning for the first time or looking to take their skills further.

Whether it's learning to float, mastering freestyle, or preparing for ocean waves, our sessions are personalized to your needs. We offer private, group, and open water lessons to match your comfort level and goals.

With our hands-on and encouraging approach, we make sure every swimmer progresses with confidence — one splash at a time.

Adult Programs

Our adult programs are designed for swimmers of all skill levels, from beginners learning fundamentals to experienced athletes training for endurance and performance.

We specialize in open water training, wave survival techniques, form improvement, and confidence building — perfect for ocean swimmers and triathletes.

Beginner swimmers are welcome to take lessons at their own pool or at our pool located in the North of Montana area in Santa Monica, depending on availability. Whether you're just starting out or looking to level up, we’ll meet you where you’re comfortable and help you build confidence at your own pace
